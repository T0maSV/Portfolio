WHO HE IS ?

Thomas VIALON

19 years old

Thomas VIALON is a student in Network and Telecommunication who learns how to manage and install a complete security network for companies combining computer sciences and networks properties. Thomas knows that Network and Telecommunication is very important for our digital future and his work contributed to it.

Thomas has been an ambassador during his preceding school for CLS company. He discover the workplace and worked for the communication between school and company resulting in a promotional video. This experience was very interesting and new for him, he acquired skills in communication. Currently, he is studying in Network and Telecommunication in the time of 3 years with the aim of entering in an engineering school in Internet Of Things.

Thomas holds a general baccalaureate specialities mathematics and sciences from the high school of Châteaudun where he got high honours.

When he is not behind computer or managing network cable, Thomas enjoys sports such as running or sailing, it gains to him perseverance and teamwork. He also likes getting with programs about new technology and watch videos on it.

Thomas is a tranquil and serious student. He is flexible to different situation and he doesn't like to be bored. He acquired problem-solving skills during his preceding school such as troubleshooting and resourcefulness.
